A recovery plan for everyone, everywhere

If we can get vaccines to everyone who needs them; get children back in classrooms, get food back on the table for everyone; recommit to protecting our planet, and make serious progress on acheiving better equity, we will be in a much stronger place to achieve the Global Goals by 2030. Without it, we will fail.

Global Citizen's white paper, "Five Critical Steps to Recover from COVID-19 Better, Together" takes a deep dive into the issues we need to work together to solve.

End Covid-19 for All

Ensuring equitable access to COVID-19 test, treatments, and vaccines for everyone, everywhere.

A true recovery from COVID-19 will only happen once everyone, everywhere has access to testing, treatment and vaccines. It is critical that the worldâ€™s richest countries ensure the world's poorest has access to these, by supporting COVAX and the Act-A, donating surplus vaccine supply, and supporting vaccine manufacturing efforts. And people everywhere must be confidence in the vaccine and get it as soon as they have access.

How do we do this?

  • Increased funding for Act-A and COVAX to ensure every country can access vaccines.
  • Wealthy countries need to donate excess doses to poorer countries to tamp down variant strains.
  • Equitable access to COVID-19 vaccines, treatments and diagnostics is made available to everyone, everywhere.


Protect the Planet

Glaciers are melting, water is rising, and the world's poorest people are being impacted the most.

We know that the 20 richest countries are responsible for 80% of carbon emissions. But it is the. most marginalized people around the world that are bearing the brunt of our planet's destruction. We must restart climate action before the consequences are too severe to recover from.

How do we do this?

  • Increased climate financing
  • Support low-carbon economies
  • Equitable access to COVID-19 vaccines, treatments and diagnostics is made available to everyone, everywhere.
